Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These machines are programmed to perform tasks 
that typically require human cognitive functions, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. 
AI is a broad field with multiple branches, including machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics.

Machine learning, a branch of AI, focuses on developing algorithms that allow computers to learn and improve from experience. 
This process enables computers to perform complex tasks without explicit programming. Another area, computer vision, allows machines to analyze 
and interpret images, identifying objects and recognizing patterns.

In industries, artificial intelligence is applied in automation, customer service, and predictive maintenance. 
For instance, chatbots equipped with AI assist in customer interactions, while predictive maintenance uses AI to forecast equipment failures 
based on data. The growing interest in artificial intelligence stems from its ability to enhance efficiency, drive innovation, and create smarter systems.
